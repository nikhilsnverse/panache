Welcome To The World Of ALIGNERS

Modern, nearly invisible orthodontic treatment

Aligners are a modern, nearly invisible orthodontic treatment (transparent plastic trays) used to straighten teeth without the traditional look of metal braces.

They consist of a series of custom-made, transparent plastic trays that fit snugly over your teeth, applying gentle, consistent pressure to move them into their ideal positions.

What Are Clear Aligners?

Clear aligners are transparent, removable trays designed to gradually move your teeth into the desired position.

  • ✓
    Custom-made using digital scans
  • ✓
    Worn 20–22 hours per day
  • ✓
    Replaced every 1–2 weeks
  • ✓
    Ideal for mild to moderate alignment issues
  • ✓
    Modern alternative to traditional braces, offering a more discreet and comfortable treatment option

Invisalign is the best known brand in the world for clear aligners.

What Is Invisalign?

Invisalign is the most advanced clear aligner system in the world, developed by Align Technology.

A leading orthodontic system using custom-made, clear, removable aligners — made from patented SmartTrack material — to straighten teeth without metal wires or brackets.

Suitable for all ages, it treats mild to complex cases, often faster than traditional braces, with visible results in as little as 6 months.

How is this possible?

iTero Lumina Scanner + Invisalign Treatment

Invisalign primarily accepts scans from iTero® intraoral scanners, now available at Panache Dental, which are developed by Align Technology, the parent company of Invisalign. iTero scanners offer the tightest integration, including the Invisalign Outcome Simulator for showing patients "before and after" results instantly.

iTero scanners are designed to integrate seamlessly with Invisalign's ClinCheck software.

They enable immediate submission, outcome simulation, and progress tracking of Invisalign cases which becomes very helpful for us as well as the patients.
